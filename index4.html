Framework

  • Main content is the tree.
  • Headings are nodes.
  • Heading levels are the level of nodes on the tree.
  • Lists provide information about nodes.
  • A checkbox hides irrelevant parts of the tree.
  • Links provide the ability to move between steps, as well as the ability to jump between nodes.
  • Each step is a different webpage.
